Football is a game played with a round image called a ball. It is played by a team of 11 on each side and it can be a friendly or competitive contest. It is a sport that unites people.
It is played on a field with green grass which has two goal posts at each end. There are goalkeepers on the 11 of each team which serve as protectors in these goal posts.
Each football team has a group of coaches that aid to train and develop them. They groom them to increase their skills for better knowledge of the sport and interesting gameplay.
